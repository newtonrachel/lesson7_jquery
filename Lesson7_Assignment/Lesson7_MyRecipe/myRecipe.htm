Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 c milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3/4 c sugar
  • 2 eggs (at room temperature)
  • 2 c flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375.

Line muffin cups with papers.

Cream butter and sugar till light and fluffy.

Beat in eggs, one at a time.

Add flour, mixed with baking powder and salt.

Alternating with milk, beat well.

Stir in vanilla.

Divide evenly into pans, bake for 18 minutes.
